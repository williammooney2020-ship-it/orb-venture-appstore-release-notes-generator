Apple release notes tips

  • Lead with the biggest change — users scan the first line to decide whether to update
  • Be specific — "Redesigned home screen with 40% faster load times" beats "Various improvements"
  • Bug fixes go last — Apple reviewers and editorial teams expect features first
  • Keep it under 300 chars for the visible snippet (the rest is behind "more")
  • Active voice only — "We added…" or "Added…", never "This update features…"
  • Avoid marketing language — "amazing", "revolutionary", "best" → flagged by reviewers
